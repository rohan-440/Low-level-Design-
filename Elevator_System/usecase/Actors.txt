Actors:

1. Primary Actors:
   Passengers:
   a. Call the Elevator by pressing up/down
   b. select the destination floor by pressing then in-car panel
   c. Emergency stops
   d. To open/close the door when idle state

2. Secondary Actors:
    Operator:
    a. Enter Maintenaince mode 
    b. Exit Maintenaince mode
    c. Acknowledge/resolved alerts modes

3. System :
    Elevator:
    a. Move up/down or idle
    b. Update display realtime
    c. detect overloading/sign alarm
    d. notify Operator
